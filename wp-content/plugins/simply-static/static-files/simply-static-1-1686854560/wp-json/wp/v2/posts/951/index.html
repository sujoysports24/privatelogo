Rabat – Real Madrid Head coach Carlo Ancelotti reportedly wants Moroccan footballer Achraf Hakimi to rejoin the Los Blancos.<\/p>\n

Sports news outlet Mundo Deportivo reported today that Ancelotti is seeking to reinforce Real Madrid\u2019s right back and striker positions.<\/p>\n

The outlet described Hakimi as an \u201cideal player\u201d for Madrid, stressing the start of his career at the Real Madrid academy, as well as the time he spent at Borussia Dortmund, Inter Milan, and most recently Paris Saint-Germain (PSG).<\/p>\n

Mundo Deportivo also reported that Hakimi is seeking an exit from PSG in the summer transfer window, but that his contract and transfer fee clause could be an obstacle for a potential move to his dream club Real Madrid.<\/p>\n

Hakimi joined PSG in the summer of 2021 under a five-year contract, which will expire in June 2026.<\/p>\n

Recent reports have, however, linked him with a potential exit from the French club, where he reportedly feels uncomfortable.<\/p>\n

One of the potential factors is \u201cthe environment and atmosphere that surrounds the French club,\u201d Mundo Deportivo reported in May, recalling the sexual assault allegations facing the Moroccan footballer.<\/p>\n

In May French outlet L\u2019Equipe claimed that the Atlas Lion dreams about rejoining Los Blancos despite speculation surrounding PSG blocking such a move.<\/p>\n

Other news outlets like AS suggested that Hakimi\u2019s level of performance this year brought him \u201cmuch criticism\u201d from within the club.<\/p>\n

The new outlet suggested that Hakimi\u2019s friendship with PSG star Kylian Mbappe is one of the \u201creasons for Achraf to be considered \u2018untouchable.\u2019\u201d<\/p>\n

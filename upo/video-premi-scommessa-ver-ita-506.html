The 2023 BET Awards 2023 will celebrate 50 years of Hip Hop this Sunday, June 25 starting at 8 p.m. ET. Patti LaBelle will hit the stage to pay tribute to Tina Turner. LaBelle will honor the incomparable Queen of Rock ‘N’ Roll, showcasing her incredible talent and capturing the essence of Turner’s unparalleled legacy in a performance that promises to be nothing short of extraordinary.
